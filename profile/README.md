## 🎯 Our Mission

Earth observation is powerful — and locked behind a paywall.

Across Africa, the institutions that most need satellite intelligence — to monitor deforestation, detect illegal mining, track crop health and map urban growth — are forced to choose between expensive proprietary software with per-seat licences, or fragmented scripts that are hard to reproduce and impossible to audit.

**EOCoreINT closes that gap.**

We build open-source tools that give African institutions the power to monitor their own land from space — without depending on costly foreign software.

## 🛠️ Our Tools

### [PyGeoFetch](https://github.com/EOCoreINT/pygeofetch)
**Universal Satellite Data Pipeline**

Unified access to 22+ satellite data providers through a single CLI and Python API.

- ✅ 22+ providers (Copernicus, USGS, NASA, Planet, Maxar, and more)
- ✅ Sentinel-1C/D SLC support
- ✅ 41-step chainable processing pipeline
- ✅ 17 spectral indices
- ✅ SAR processing (despeckle, calibrate, flood mapping)
- ✅ YAML pipeline orchestration
- ✅ 70+ integration tests
- 📦 `pip install pygeofetch`

---

### [PyGeoVision](https://github.com/EOCoreINT/pygeovision)
**World-Class Geospatial AI Platform**

Production-ready platform unifying satellite data, AI models, and natural language interaction.

- ✅ 119 model architectures
- ✅ DINOv3 (12 variants) + Prithvi-EO-2.0
- ✅ Natural language agent (heuristic + LLM)
- ✅ Complete InSAR processing chain
- ✅ 20+ spectral indices
- ✅ Interactive visualization
- ✅ 944+ tests
- 📦 `pip install pygeovision`
